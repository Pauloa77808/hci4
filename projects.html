DiKA - Digital Kiosk for Anamnesis

The DiKA is a technological solution to improve both the time and quality of the anamnesis procedure performed by medical staff in the emergency department. The introduction of a self-service kiosk in the emergency department waiting room will make it possible to quickly and intuitively collect the patientâ€™s past medical history, usual medication, main complaint symptoms and vital signs. The primary purpose of this solution is to reduce the period of clinical observation and thus improve the response capacity of the emergency department with the same resources.
