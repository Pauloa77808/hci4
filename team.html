Nuno Feixa Rodrigues
Principal Investigator
nfr@ipca.pt
+351 929 145 931
Nuno Rodrigues has extended research activity at the different areas of this PhD proposal, in particular in formal methods, software analysis, medical systems and serious games for health. Nuno Rodrigues is a researcher with a base education in Computer Science and a PhD in Software Engineering. Since 2009 he has been a Professor at the School of Technology of IPCA, where he has held several positions, namely as Digital Games Development Engineering Degree Director. In 2010, together with other colleagues he founded the DIGARC research unit, the first Portuguese research unit specifically dedicated to the research of digital games and its applications to health, having later evolved to the current research unit 2AI. ​
